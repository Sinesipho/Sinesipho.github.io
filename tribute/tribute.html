Career

She began her career as a fashion model at the age of 16 and did contract work with a modeling agency, Elite Model Management.

For the next year, she worked all over the world on contracts for companies including Calvin Klein and Levi's. When she was 17, she was featured on the
cover of the July 1990 issue of Seventeen. Diaz also modeled for 2 to 3 months in Australia and shot a commercial for Coca-Cola in Sydney in 1991.
